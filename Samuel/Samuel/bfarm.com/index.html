Bowles Farming Company

A foundation built on six generations of California family farming.

Inspiring growth, fostering sustainability.

Transparency, integrity, and a strong work ethic are core values. We take great pride in our contributions to both the community and the environment. We will create a better world by leaving our land in a better condition today for the generations to come tomorrow.

Restore, Enhance, Protect

We are dedicated to cultivating the land with an unwavering commitment to the well-being of the people, the soil, our customers, and the environment.

Our ongoing research shows promising signs as we evaluate carbon sequestration opportunities and other efforts toward reducing our environmental impact.

Water Conservation and Sustainable Energy Initiatives

We continue to prioritize water conservation, exemplified by our drip irrigation installation, greatly reducing our applied water. Solar panels (shown here) generate renewable power for our drip irrigation systems that reduce water usage.

Our Bowles Farming Family

Our team members are more than just employees, they’re part of our family. We invest in our employees, and community, because we know that they are the heart and soul of our business, and we believe that our shared commitment to each other is what makes us strong.
